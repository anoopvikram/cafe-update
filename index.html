About Us

Welcome to Thorn n' Petals! Established in 2020, our cozy cafe has quickly become a beloved spot in Thrissur. We offer a blend of delicious food, refreshing drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere for people to connect and create memories. At Thorn n' Petals, we believe food is about more than just filling your stomach—it's about sharing joyful moments. Whether you're grabbing a quick breakfast, enjoying a leisurely lunch, or sipping on a freshly brewed coffee, we have something for everyone. Our menu features a variety of wholesome options, from hearty burgers to mouthwatering desserts.
